Membership

An invitation to drive with us.

Membership is through PCA National. When you join PCA and live in Fresno, Kings, Madera, or Tulare county, you are automatically a member of the Sequoia Region. Welcome.

i.

Join PCA National

Sign up at pca.org. Annual membership covers you and a family co-member.

ii.

Pick Sequoia

Choose Sequoia as your home region during sign up. Or call the chapter and we will help.

iii.

Show Up

Come to a cars and coffee. Bring the Porsche. You will know everyone by the second event.

What is included

What membership gives you.

Region Events

Cars and coffee twice a month, a monthly dinner, driving tours, and autocross days.

Zone 7 Events

Access to all ten Zone 7 region calendars across Northern and Central California.

National PCA

Panorama magazine, member discounts, national events, classifieds, and more.

The Region Itself

A community of Porsche owners across the Central Valley. The cars are the door. The people are the reason.

Ready to drive with us

Join PCA. Get Sequoia.

Membership opens through PCA National. The whole process takes about five minutes online. Once you are in, you are in for the year. We hope to see you at the next cars and coffee.
