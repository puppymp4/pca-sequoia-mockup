Welcome

Porsche owners and the roads they were built for.

Our region serves the Greater Central San Joaquin Valley, the natural amphitheater for the Giant Sequoia groves along the western slopes of the Sierra Nevada. We meet for coffee, we drive together, and we share what we love about the cars and the people who own them.
