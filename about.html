The Region

It is not just the cars. It is the people.

Founded in 1958, the Sequoia Region is one of ten chapters in PCA Zone 7. We are a Central Valley club run by volunteers, built around the people who love the cars.

Who we are

The Central Valley chapter.

Our region serves Porsche owners in the Greater Central San Joaquin Valley of California, the natural amphitheater for the Giant Sequoia groves along the western slopes of the Sierra Nevada mountains. That covers Fresno, Kings, Madera, and Tulare counties.

We are one of ten regions in PCA Zone 7, which runs across Northern and Central California. Membership is open to anyone who owns a Porsche or is on the path to owning one. Family members are welcome at every event.

The chapter is run by an all volunteer Board of Directors. Members elect the board, the board writes the calendar, and the rest of us show up. That is the whole model. It has worked for decades and it still works today.
